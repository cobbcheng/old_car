    • Shipment



    Every year the Organizing Committee appoints a logistics partner for 4C. Due to the constant fluctuation of shipping prices, the name of this year’s partner and shipping costs from different ports around the world will be disclosed in July 2016. Our partner will work in compliance with a set of strict rules and ensure outstanding service. Depending on their own needs, participants may choose among different options, such as delivering their vehicle to the appointed port and entrust it to the forwarder’s care or having it collected directly at their door.

    Of course, those with sound knowledge or good relations in the logistic field may choose to ship their car through a forwarder of their choice; this is fully authorized by the Organizing Committee. In this case, participants must request our “Guidelines for Autonomous Shipment” and the forwarder will need to provide the required information so that the Organizing Committee can complete all on-site procedures.

    Regardless of the shipping method, vehicles must arrive in China by 15 September 2016 to allow sufficient time for customs clearance. On the basis of this deadline, shipment should take place by the end of July or the beginning of August 2016.
